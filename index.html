About

What is Junction Lisbon?

An international 36-hour hackathon where teams of 1 to 5 people will work on technology projects with real-world impact. Participants will choose from challenges set by partner companies and institutions, focusing on areas such as:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Technology Serving Society
  • Urban Sustainability and Mobility
  • Finance and Market Technologies

Frequently asked questions

What are the benefits of the hackathon?
— Build fast, learn from mentors, network with partners, get feedback and win prizes.

— Come solo or form a team on-site. Both are welcome.
Will there be a remote option?
— The event is in-person in Lisbon for this edition.
Do I need coding experience?
— Technical and non‑technical roles are welcome. Projects need at least one technical member.
